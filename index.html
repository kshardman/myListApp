Privacy Policy

myListApp respects your privacy.

The app does not collect, track, or transmit any personal information.

All lists, items, and settings are stored locally on your device using Apple’s system storage. Your data never leaves your device unless you explicitly choose to share it using iOS share features such as Messages, Mail, or Files.

The app:

  • Does not use analytics
  • Does not use advertising
  • Does not track usage
  • Does not create user accounts
  • Does not send any data to external servers

Data Usage

myListApp does not collect or process any user data.

Any data you create (lists and items) remains on your device and is only shared if you choose to export or send it.
